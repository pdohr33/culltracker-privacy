CullTracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-15

Summary

CullTracker is an offline bass fishing tournament tracking app. The app stores your session data and settings on your device only. The app shows ads using Google AdMob.

Data we collect

  • Local app data: fish weights, session history, personal records, and settings are stored on your device only.
  • Advertising data: the app uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and basic diagnostics to serve and measure ads.

How we use data

  • To save your fishing sessions, history, and settings locally.
  • To display and measure ads (via AdMob).

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Advertising data may be processed by Google as part of AdMob ad serving and measurement.

Children

CullTracker is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

Email: Culltracker@gmail.com

Changes

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date.
